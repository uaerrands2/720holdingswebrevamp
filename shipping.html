Shipping Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Shipping Destinations

We ship to all counties in Kenya. Delivery times and costs vary depending on your location:

  • Nairobi: 1-2 business days (Free for orders over KES 10,000)
  • Central Region (Kiambu, Thika, Nyeri, Murang'a): 2-3 business days (KES 500-800)
  • Coastal Region (Mombasa, Lamu, Kilifi): 3-4 business days (KES 1,000-1,500)
  • Western Region (Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret): 3-5 business days (KES 800-1,200)
  • Other Regions: 5-7 business days (Contact for quote)

2. Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days (included for orders over KES 10,000 in Nairobi)
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days (KES 1,500 additional)
  • Store Pickup: Ready in 24 hours at our Nairobi warehouse (Free)

3. Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive:

  • Tracking number via email and SMS
  • Real-time tracking updates
  • Delivery confirmation
  • Driver contact information

4. Delivery Confirmation

Our delivery partners will:

  • Call you 1-2 hours before arrival
  • Provide professional delivery services
  • Require signature upon delivery for large items
  • Inspect items for damage before delivery

5. Failed Delivery Attempts

If our delivery partner cannot reach you:

  • We'll attempt delivery at least twice
  • We'll contact you to reschedule
  • Items will be held for 7 days
  • After 7 days, items may be returned to us

6. Damaged Goods

If your items arrive damaged:

  1. Document the damage with photos
  2. Do NOT refuse the delivery (document as "damaged")
  3. Contact us within 24 hours with photos and order number
  4. We'll arrange replacement or refund immediately

7. Large Items & Installation

For large orders (curtain rods, hardware kits, etc.):

  • We offer professional delivery to your installation site
  • Professional installation available for additional fee
  • Installation scheduling required 48 hours after delivery
  • Our technicians provide after-delivery support

8. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Order weight and dimensions
  • Delivery location
  • Shipping method selected
  • Special handling requirements (if any)

9. Lost or Missing Items

If your order doesn't arrive:

  • Check tracking for delivery confirmation
  • Contact our support team immediately
  • We'll investigate with the courier
  • Replacement or refund will be provided

10. Special Orders & Bulk Shipping

For bulk orders or special projects:

  • Contact our sales team for custom quotes
  • We arrange specialized shipping
  • Dedicated logistics support
  • Flexible delivery scheduling

11. International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within Kenya. For international inquiries, please contact us at sales@seventwentyholdings.co.ke.
