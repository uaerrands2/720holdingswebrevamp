Returns & Refunds Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Return Period

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy for all products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange within 30 days of purchase.

2. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all accessories
  • Within 30 days of purchase
  • Not installation services or special orders

3. How to Initiate a Return

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our returns department at returns@seventwentyholdings.co.ke or 0700 720 720
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Receive your return shipping label
  4. Pack your item securely and ship it back
  5. We'll process your refund within 5-7 business days of receiving the return

4. Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund to your original payment method. Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to appear in your account, depending on your financial institution.

5. Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please:

  • Report the issue within 7 days of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage
  • We'll arrange a replacement or refund at no cost to you

6. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Installation services
  • Custom orders
  • Items not in original condition
  • Items purchased on sale or clearance (store credit only)
  • Safety equipment used for installation

7. Product Warranties

Our products include the following warranties:

  • Curtain Rods: 20-year warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Paints: 10-year warranty on color and coverage
  • Hardware: 5-year warranty against defects
  • Solar Products: 3-year warranty on panels and electronics

8. Warranty Claims

Warranty claims must be made within the warranty period. Defects due to normal wear and tear, misuse, or improper installation are not covered by warranty.

9. Return Shipping

SevenTwenty Holdings will provide a prepaid return shipping label for items within our return policy. For items outside the return period, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

10. Contact Us

For any questions about returns or refunds:

  • Email: returns@seventwentyholdings.co.ke
  • Phone: 0700 720 720
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM (EAT)
