Our Story & Mission

Four subsidiaries united by a commitment to quality, innovation, and Kenyan manufacturing excellence

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leader

Founded in 2010, SevenTwenty Holdings began as a small curtain rod manufacturing operation in Kiambu. Recognizing the growing demand for quality building materials in Kenya's construction boom, we expanded our portfolio to include paints, hardware, and solar solutions.

Today, we're proud to operate four specialized subsidiaries that serve homeowners, contractors, and businesses across Kenya. Our growth is rooted in a simple philosophy: manufacture durable, affordable products locally to support Kenya's economy and infrastructure development.

Our Subsidiaries

Four specialized brands, one commitment to quality

Executive Curtain Rods

Founded: 2010

Specialty: Premium powder-coated curtain rods, tracks, and accessories

Unique Feature: 20+ year lifespan guarantee

Manufactured in our Kiambu facility using corrosion-resistant steel specifically formulated for Kenya's climate.

SetPaints

Founded: 2014

Specialty: Interior, exterior, and specialty paints

Unique Feature: Low-VOC, mold-resistant formulas

Our Kahawa plant produces premium paints tested against humidity, UV, and temperature variations common in Kenya.

Set Hardware

Founded: 2017

Specialty: Door and window hardware, building accessories

Unique Feature: Complete installation kits

Located in Nairobi's Gikomba area, we supply contractors and DIY enthusiasts with durable hardware solutions.

SunWatch Solar

Founded: 2020

Specialty: Solar lighting and power solutions

Unique Feature: Motion-sensor technology

Providing affordable, reliable solar solutions to combat Kenya's electricity challenges and reduce energy costs.
