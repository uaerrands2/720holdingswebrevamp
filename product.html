Installation Guide

1

Measure & Mark

Measure the width of your window and mark the installation points on the wall.

2

Drill Holes

Drill holes at the marked points using a 6mm masonry drill bit.

3

Install Brackets

Insert wall plugs and screw the brackets securely into place.

4

Mount Rod

Place the curtain rod onto the brackets and secure with provided screws.
