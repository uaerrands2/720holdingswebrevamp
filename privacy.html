Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

SevenTwenty Holdings ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the site includes:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, shipping address, billing address, and order history
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, M-Pesa information, and bank account details (processed securely)
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, and links clicked

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To process your orders and deliver your products
  • To send you order confirmations and updates
  • To improve our website and services
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To send promotional emails (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Delivery partners for order fulfillment
  • Payment processors to process transactions
  • Service providers who assist us in operating our website
  • Law enforcement when required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Data portability

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser preferences.
